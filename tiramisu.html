Tiramisu

Description

This classic Tiramisu is made authentically in the Italian way, with espresso soaked ladyfingers layered with a light and airy mascarpone cream, and dusted with cocoa powder to finish. This is a great make ahead dessert and perfect for entertaining!

Ingredients

  • 3 egg whites
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3 +3 tbsp sugar
  • 8 oz mascarpone cheese at room temperature
  • 1 cup freshly pulled espresso cooled to room temperature
  • 2 tbsp amaretto or spiced rum
  • 3-4 dozen ladyfingers storebought or homemade
  • cocoa powder for dusting

Steps

    Step 1

  1. In a clean bowl, whip the egg whites and 3 tbsp of sugar together with a hand mixer, for about 3-5 minutes until the egg whites hold stiff peaks.

    2. Step 2

  2. In a separate bowl, whip the egg yolks with the remaining 3 tbsp sugar for 2-3 minutes until the egg yolks are thick and pale yellow in color.

    3. Step 3

  3. Add the mascarpone to the egg yolks and whip until combined.

    4. Step 4

  4. Gently fold the stiff egg whites into the egg yolk mixture and set aside.

    5. Step 5

  5. In a small flat dish or bowl, combine the espresso and amaretto.

    6. Step 6

  6. Dunk each ladyfinger fully into the espresso mixture for 1-2 seconds and place into the bottom of a 8x8 dish, or into individual ramekins. Donâ€™t let the ladyfinger soak so much that it falls apart, just a quick dunk to let it absorb a little bit of espresso.

    7. Step 7

  7. Once the ladyfingers have formed a single layer in the bottom of the dish, spread 1/2 of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.

    8. Step 8

  8. Arrange another layer of espresso soaked ladyfingers on top, and spread over the remaining mascarpone cream.

    9. Step 9

  9. Cover the top of the dish with plastic wrap and let the tiramisu refrigerate for 4-6 hours.

    10. Step 10

  10. Serve cold, with a light dusting of cocoa on top. Enjoy!
